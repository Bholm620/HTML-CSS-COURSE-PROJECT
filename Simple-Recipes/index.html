Featured Recipe

Pizza

Prep Time

30-40 minutes

Level

medium

Deep dish pizza has got a lot going for it: a thick crust that's tender and chewy on the inside, but nicely crisped on the outside; a little extra room for piling on the toppings; and, deep dish is less needy than its thin crust style counterpart, which requires some care when rolling and stretching. Here, you just push the dough into the skillet and you're cooking. Save the leftovers for lunch!

About Us

Simple Recipes is a website dedicated to providing easy and delicious recipes for everyone.

We believe that cooking should be simple and enjoyable, which is why we focus on recipes that are easy to follow and use common ingredients. Our goal is to help you create delicious meals without the stress.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, we have something for everyone. Join us on our culinary journey and discover the joy of cooking!
